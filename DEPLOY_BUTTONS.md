## After Deployment

1. Wait 2-5 minutes for the app to build
2. Your app will be available at a URL like:
   - Render: `https://hide-seek-3d-xxxx.onrender.com`
   - Railway: `https://hide-seek-3d.up.railway.app`
   - Glitch: `https://hide-seek-3d.glitch.me`

3. Share this URL with friends - **no sign-up required for players!**

4. Test by:
   - Opening the URL
   - Creating a room (enter your name)
   - Opening in another browser/device
   - Joining with the room code
   - Testing voice chat and gameplay

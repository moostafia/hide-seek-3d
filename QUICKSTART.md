## Option 1: Render (Permanent - Recommended) ‚≠ê

### 3-Step Deployment:

1. **Sign up at [render.com](https://render.com)**
   - Free account, no credit card needed

2. **Create Web Service**
   - Click "New +" button
   - Select "Web Service"
   - Connect your GitHub: `moostafia/hide-seek-3d`
   - Render auto-detects the configuration

3. **Deploy & Share**
   - Click "Create Web Service"
   - Wait 5 minutes
   - Copy your URL: `https://hide-seek-3d-xxxx.onrender.com`
   - Share with friends!

**Done! Your game is live. No sign-up needed for players.**

## Troubleshooting:

**App won't load?**
- Wait 30 seconds if on Render free tier (app may be sleeping)
- Check if URL starts with `https://`

**Voice chat not working?**
- Ensure you're using HTTPS (all platforms provide this)
- Allow microphone permissions in browser
- Test on Chrome or Safari

**Can't connect to room?**
- Refresh the page
- Try creating a new room
- Check browser console for errors
